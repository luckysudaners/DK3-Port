About me

Front End Developer

Hi! I’m Dan, a self-taught software engineer from Saint Paul, Minnesota. I got into coding in 2018 when I first made a couple of basic web applications in Java. Once Covid hit, I jumped into the deep end of web development and never looked back. I’ve always loved problem solving and coding is the perfect avenue to channel my passion and curiosity. I chose each project to push my skill set to the next level all while providing a fun challenge of creating something new. This website was built to highlight the skills I’ve learned and the projects I’ve constructed over the last year. What I love most about coding is how the problems and challenges never end. There’s always going to be something new to learn or a problem to tackle. The satisfaction and sense of accomplishment from solving those problems is what drives me to further my coding skills.

In my free time, I love to go hiking and explore MN’s gorgeous state parks. When I’m not hiking, I’m continually trying to find the best taco spots in the city, it’s hard work but somebody’s gotta do it. I’m also a big stand up comedy fan and love going to ACME.
